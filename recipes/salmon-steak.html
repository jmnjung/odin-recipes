Salmon Steak

Description

Salmon steak is a flavorful and versatile dish that is perfect for both casual meals and special occasions. This dish features thick, juicy cuts of salmon that are pan-seared to achieve a crispy exterior while keeping the interior tender and succulent. The rich, slightly sweet flavor of the salmon is enhanced with a simple seasoning of salt and pepper, and a touch of lemon juice adds a refreshing citrus note that complements the fish beautifully.

For an added layer of depth, the salmon steaks can be served with a side of garlic butter or a fresh herb sauce. This dish pairs wonderfully with a variety of sides, from roasted vegetables to a light salad, making it a great choice for a balanced and satisfying meal. Whether you are cooking for a family dinner or entertaining guests, salmon steak is sure to impress with its ease of preparation and elegant presentation.

Ingredients

  • Two salmon steaks
  • Two tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • A lemon
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • Two tablespoons of fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Cut the lemon into wedges, and finely chop the garlic and parsley.
  2. Pat the steaks dry with paper towels and season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  4. Once the oil is hot, add the salmon steaks to the skillet skin-side down.
  5. Cook for 4-5 minutes per side or until the salmon is cooked through with a crisp.
  6. Add minced garlic, squeeze lemon juice, and garnish with parsley.
